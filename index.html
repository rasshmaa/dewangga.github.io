A Must Visit City

Description of cities that must be visited while in Indonesia

Bali

Bali has very beautiful tourist attractions, one of which is Kuta Beach which is very popular in Indonesia and internationally. Kuta Beach has excellent facilities, namely accommodation, restaurants, bars, and world-famous surf spots. Kuta beach also provides pubs and restaurants in the evening hours. In addition to offering natural beauty, the surrounding environment also provides classy facilities

Yogyakarta

Jogja has its own uniqueness for tourist destinations, such as cultural or historical tours, architectural tours, temple tours, and monument museums. In addition, there are places for traditional crafts made of silver, wood, cloth, or leather. we can see the manufacturing process directly. Meanwhile, modern shopping centers offer branded goods at very affordable prices.

Lombok

Lombok Island is an island in the Lesser Sunda archipelago or Nusa Tenggara which is separated by the Lombok Strait from Bali to the west and the Alas Strait to the east from Sumbawa. The island is more or less round in shape with a kind of "tail" on the southwest side which is approximately 70 km long. The island's area reaches 5,435 kmÂ² placing it at number 108 of the list of islands by area in the world. The main city on this island is Mataram City.
